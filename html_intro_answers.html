JOKER

Joker is a 2019 American psychological thriller film directed by Todd Phillips from a screenplay he co-wrote with Scott Silver.Joaquin Phoenix’s performance is a big part of what makes the movie stand out.

a man who has a hard time fitting into society. He works as a clown but dreams of being a comedian. As things get worse for him, he starts losing control and becomes the villain known as the Joker.

The film focuses on how Arthur's struggles with mental health and feeling ignored by society push him to change, and became the Joker.

Main Cast

Arthur Fleck: The troubled protagonist, Arthur is a struggling comedian with a dark past. He suffers from mental illness and societal neglect, which ultimately pushes him to become the villainous Joker. His descent into madness forms the core of the film.

Murray Franklin: Played by Robert De Niro, Murray is a late-night talk show host who represents a symbol of success and approval in Arthur’s eyes. His interactions with Arthur are key in the movie, particularly when Arthur seeks recognition and validation from him.

Sophie Dumond: Sophie is a neighbor of Arthur’s who, in the beginning, seems to offer him kindness and even romantic interest. However, her role is later revealed to have been more complex, highlighting the blurred lines between Arthur’s perceptions and reality.
